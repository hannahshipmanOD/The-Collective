# Training

## Trigger phrases
- "training"
- "onboarding"
- "new hire"
- "ramp"
- "training plan"
- "how do I teach"

## What this skill does

Helps create training content, onboarding plans, and learning materials for XA and TC team members.

## Workflow

### Onboarding plan
1. Ask: what role? (XA, TC, sales support)
2. Ask: what's the start date and target ramp date?
3. Generate week-by-week onboarding plan
4. Include: systems access, shadowing schedule, key readings, checkpoints

### Training content
1. Ask: what topic or skill?
2. Ask: what format? (written guide, checklist, FAQ, quiz)
3. Generate content using domain knowledge from:
   - `skills/cx-knowledge/` for XA content
   - `skills/tc-pipeline-daily/` for TC content
   - `skills/escalation-pathways/` for escalation training

### Quiz generation
1. Take source content
2. Generate 5–10 multiple choice questions
3. Include answer key with explanations

## XA onboarding milestones

| Week | Focus |
|------|-------|
| Week 1 | Systems access, shadowing, Opendoor overview |
| Week 2 | First calls with supervisor support |
| Week 3 | Solo calls, supervisor available |
| Week 4 | Full caseload with check-ins |
| Week 8 | Ramp complete checkpoint |
