# Wavelength

## Trigger phrases
- "wavelength"
- "team pulse"
- "async update"
- "team communication"
- "check in"

## What this skill does

Helps with async team communication patterns: drafting team updates, capturing pulse on how the team is doing, writing manager notes, and structuring async standup content.

## Workflow

### Team update draft
1. Ask: what's the update about? (ops update, incident follow-up, strategic update, etc.)
2. Draft update with: context, key points, asks/actions, timeline
3. Format for Slack or email as appropriate

### Pulse capture
1. Ask: what signals have you observed? (morale, workload, blockers, wins)
2. Synthesize into: current state, risks, opportunities
3. Suggest follow-up actions

### Manager 1:1 prep
1. Load recent context (Linear issues, recent metrics)
2. Draft talking points: wins, concerns, asks, career/growth items
3. Format as 1:1 agenda

## Output principles
- Be direct and specific, not vague
- Lead with the most important thing
- Include concrete asks or next steps
- Keep Slack messages to 3 bullet points max unless complexity demands more
