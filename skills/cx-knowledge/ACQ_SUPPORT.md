# Acquisition Support Playbook

Acquisition support covers customer contacts related to buying/selling with Opendoor — from initial inquiry through close.

## XA responsibilities

- Answer inbound calls, emails, and chats from sellers
- Provide offer explanations and walk-throughs
- Coordinate assessment scheduling
- Handle inspection questions and repair negotiations
- Support closing logistics
- De-escalate when customers are frustrated

## Common contact categories

| Category | Description | Typical resolution |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| `offer_questions` | Questions about offer price/terms | Explain comp methodology, pull offer details |
| `assessment_scheduling` | Schedule or reschedule assessment | Coordinate with assessment team |
| `inspection_results` | Questions about inspection findings | Walk through report, explain repair credit |
| `closing_questions` | Closing date, docs, wire instructions | Coordinate with TC team |
| `cancellation` | Customer wants to cancel | De-escalate, explore alternatives |
| `general_inquiry` | General how-does-this-work questions | Education and expectation setting |

## Escalation triggers

Escalate to Senior XA or manager when:
- Customer is threatening legal action
- Repeat contacts (3+) on same issue without resolution
- Request to speak with manager
- Pricing dispute with factual error in our system
- Wire fraud concern

See `skills/escalation-pathways/SKILL.md` for full escalation routing.
